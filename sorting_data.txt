a: When I consider everything that grows
b: Holds in perfection but a little moment,
c: That this huge stage presenteth nought but shows
d: Whereon the stars in secret influence comment;
e: When I perceive that men as plants increase,
f: Cheered and check'd even by the selfsame sky,
g: Vaunt in their youthful sap, at height decrease,
h: And wear their brave state out of memory;
i: Then the conceit of this inconstant stay
j: Sets you most rich in youth before my sight,
k: Where wasteful Time debateth with Decay
m: To change your day of youth to sullied night;
n: And all in war with Time for love of you,
l: As he takes from you, I engraft you new.
